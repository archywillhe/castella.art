Castella is inspired by the late Leo Castelli,
the art salesman from the 20th century whose
impacts to this world remain vivid to this day.

Just like Leo, Steve, Elon, and Sam Altman,
we believe in the limitation of expressionism,
the emptiness within that which recurs ad infinitum.

Lao Tzu once said, "The ten thousand things
carry yin and embrace yang. They achieve
harmony by combining these forces."

As much as we are optimistic in the singularity,
we subscribe to the down-fall of us collectively.

Post-humanism is not a mean to an end,
